Freedom to Buy



Our mission is to lead the charge in democratizing the distribution and spending of value across the globe through the use of digital currency.

Although we primarily operate as a bridge between USD/FIAT based Merchants and the Ethereum Network, we hope to popularize cryptocurrencies for everyday use as a means of establishing widespread trust and ultimately adoption of cryptocurrency.

Blockchain technologies allow us to transact value quickly and inexpensively like never before, but being able to move representations of value around only gets us halfway there.

We must move on from the speculative nature of the digital currency market and demonstrate that cryptocurrency can be used for every day purchases such as groceries and monthly rent. Only then will the full value of blockchain technology be realized.

Features

Why Choose Verto?

Secure

Verto does not manage your Ethereum account. Simply transfer Ether between Verto's smart-contract-secured platform and your existing wallet provider.

Transparent

The framework of Verto's holding contract will be made public, letting users see how their Ether is stored and managed.

Private

Verto does not store sensitive user data, and we collect the minimum amount of information required to open a crypto card under US financial regulatory requirements.

Integrated

For tech Savvy users, Verto allows direct interaction with your debit account programmatically through the Ethereum Network.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I use Verto to purchase Ether?

    Verto does not allow the purchasing of Ether. We provide our users with a contract-backed debit account that interfaces with your Wallet provider in order to protect your unused funds.

  • Where can I get the Verto Card?

    The Verto Card is still in development, but our infrastructure will be up and running soon. Scroll to the section above for more details about the Verto Card.

  • Does Verto have control over my Ether?

    Verto is not able to access funds on your Ether debit account unless a purchase with your Verto Card is made. The only person that can access your funds is the owner of the Ethereum account that deposited Ether.

  • Do I need to share my private key with Verto?

    Verto does not need your private key or access to your Ethereum wallet. Upon launching our platform, we will provide an Ethereum contract address that you can use to upload funds directly from your Ethereum wallet.
